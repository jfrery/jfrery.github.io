About Me

I am doing a PhD in the Laboratory Hubert Curien in collaboration with Worldline (an e-services company). My research interests lie ensemble learning, boosting, meta learning, online learning and continual learning. I am curious of the state of the art and therefore dedicate some time to acquire this knowledge. Beside research, I have a strong interest in solving today's machine learning problems for real-use cases (e.g. acquiring data, making sense out of it, building SOTA ML models). Web dev is also something I do in my spare time.
